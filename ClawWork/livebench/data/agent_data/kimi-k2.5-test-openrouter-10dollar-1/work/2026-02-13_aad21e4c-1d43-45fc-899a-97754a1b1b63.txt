1. **Transaction Terms**: Alan Gane purchases 1,000,000 common shares for $500,000 ($0.50 per share)

2. **Investor Qualifications**: Confirms Alan is an accredited investor investing in his individual capacity

3. **Information and Inspection Rights**: Provides minority investor information rights (quarterly/annual financials, material events) and document inspection rights, without requiring board or observer participation

4. **Anti-Dilution Protection**: Includes weighted average anti-dilution provisions maintaining Alan's ownership at no less than 10% of fully diluted capitalization, with customary top-up rights and carve-outs for exempt issuances (employee equity, acquisitions, splits)

5. **Pre-emptive Rights**: Grants Alan pro rata participation rights in future equity issuances to maintain his ownership percentage

6. **Minority Investor Consent Rights**: Requires Alan's prior approval for: change of control transactions, liquidation/dissolution, amendments to charter/bylaws affecting his rights, indebtedness over $250,000, dividends/repurchases, material changes to business/management, and related party transactions

7. **Customary Provisions**: Includes standard representations and warranties, covenants, closing conditions, and boilerplate provisions

8. **Capitalization Schedule**: Shows pre-investment (5,000,000 shares) and post-investment (6,000,000 shares) capitalization

9. **Bracketed Placeholders**: All unknown information (dates, addresses, wire instructions) marked with [BRACKETED] placeholders for completion
