I have successfully created both required Word documents for the dialysis facility:

1. **Fax Cover Sheet**: A one-page professional fax cover document that includes:
   - Company logo and contact information from the provided reference file
   - Sender information fields (name, fax number, telephone number)
   - Recipient information fields (name, fax number, telephone number)
   - Date, subject line, and number of pages fields
   - Priority options: Urgent, For Review, Please Comment, Please Reply
   - Confidentiality statement from the provided reference document
   - Professional formatting with clear sections

2. **Patient Admission Pre-Screening Checklist**: A comprehensive two-page checklist that includes:
   - Company logo and document identification as "Facility Admission: Pre-Screening Checklist"
   - Patient name and date of birth fields at the top of each page
   - Complete table with all required patient information elements from the reference document:
     * Patient Face Sheet/Demographics
     * Proof of Insurance
     * Hepatitis B Status
     * Recent Lab Results
     * Vaccination records (Influenza, Pneumonia)
     * TB Screening (PPD or Chest X-Ray)
     * EKG
     * History & Physical
     * Current medications
     * Allergies
     * Physician Orders
     * Vascular Access Operative Report
     * Optional: Hospital discharge summary, hemodialysis flowsheets, 2728 form
     * Interdisciplinary Assessments and Care Plan
     * Transportation information
   - Columns for Date Sent, Date Received, and Initials
   - Clear indication that Date Received and Initials are completed by Internal Dialysis Facility Staff Only
   - Footer with page numbers (Page 1 of 2, Page 2 of 2)
   - Contact information and instructions about the 48-hour review process
   - All formatting meets professional healthcare documentation standards

Both documents adhere to regulatory compliance requirements and are designed to assist the administrative staff in gathering necessary patient information efficiently.
