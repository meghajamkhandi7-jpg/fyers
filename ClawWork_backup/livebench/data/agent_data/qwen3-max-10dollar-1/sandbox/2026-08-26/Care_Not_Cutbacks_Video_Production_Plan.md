## Stock Footage Recommendations

Based on the script requirements, the following types of royalty-free stock footage should be sourced from platforms like Shutterstock, Pond5, or iStock:

1. **Children with Autism**: Authentic, respectful footage showing children in therapeutic or family settings
2. **Healthcare Settings**: Medical professionals working with children, therapy sessions
3. **Family Moments**: Parents/caregivers supporting children with special needs
4. **Emotional Close-ups**: Genuine expressions of concern, hope, and determination

**Technical Requirements for Stock Footage:**
- Minimum resolution: 1920x1080 (Full HD)
- Frame rate: 24fps or higher
- Format: MP4, MOV, or similar professional formats
- License: Royalty-free with commercial usage rights

**Visual Treatment:**
- Apply desaturation (-30% to -50% saturation)
- Darken exposure (-0.3 to -0.7 stops)
- Add subtle vignette for dramatic effect
- Slow motion up to 50% where appropriate for emotional impact
- Reposition/zoom to create dynamic movement

## Music Selection Recommendations

**Requirements:**
- Dramatic, emotionally evocative instrumental track
- Clear beginning and strong ending
- No vocals (to avoid competing with supers)
- Appropriate for serious social/political messaging
- Royalty-free with commercial license

**Recommended Sources:**
- Pond5's "Dramatic Emotional" or "Social Issues" categories
- AudioJungle's "Cinematic Drama" collections  
- PremiumBeat's "Serious Documentary" tracks

**Technical Specifications:**
- Edit down to exactly 15 seconds
- Ensure smooth fade-in at start and impactful ending
- Maintain consistent emotional build throughout
- Mix levels appropriately with any ambient sound from footage

## Technical Export Specifications

**Final Output Requirements:**
- Format: H.264 (.mp4)
- Resolution: 1920x1080 (16:9 aspect ratio)
- Duration: Exactly 15.00 seconds
- Frame Rate: 24fps (or match source footage)
- Bitrate: High quality (minimum 10 Mbps)
- Color Space: Rec. 709
- Audio: AAC stereo, 48kHz, 320kbps

## Text Super Specifications

**Font Requirements:**
- Highly legible sans-serif font (e.g., Helvetica Bold, Arial Black, or similar)
- Large font size ensuring readability on all screens
- High contrast against backgrounds (white text with subtle drop shadow)
- Consistent styling across all supers

**Timing Guidelines:**
- Each super must remain on screen long enough for comfortable reading
- Minimum display time: 2 seconds per super
- Smooth transitions between supers (cross-dissolve recommended)
- Tag line appears as two separate elements with clear separation

## Final Notes

This production plan ensures the commercial meets all client requirements while maintaining the dramatic, urgent tone necessary to convey the seriousness of potential coverage cuts for children with autism. The pacing allows viewers sufficient time to read each super while maintaining emotional impact through carefully selected visuals and music.

The client should source high-quality, authentic stock footage that respectfully represents children with autism and their families. The visual treatment (desaturation, darkening, slow motion) will transform potentially bright stock footage into the moody, serious tone required for this advocacy message.
