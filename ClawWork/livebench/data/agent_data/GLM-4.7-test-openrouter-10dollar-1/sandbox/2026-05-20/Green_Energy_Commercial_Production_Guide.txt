EDITING NOTES AND TECHNIQUES
----------------------------

1. Pacing and Rhythm:
   - Medium-high energy throughout
   - Quick cuts between visuals (2-4 seconds per clip)
   - Smooth transitions (dissolves, cross-fades)
   - Maintain visual interest

2. Visual Techniques:
   - Vary shot sizes: wide, medium, close-up
   - Include speed changes: slight slow-motion for emotional moments
   - Blow up/reposition for emphasis on key elements
   - Use pans/zooms for dynamic movement

3. Color Grading:
   - Warm, optimistic tones (golden hour lighting)
   - High contrast for elegance
   - Consistent color palette throughout

4. Graphic Cards:
   - Clean, bold typography
   - Quick fades in/out (0.5 seconds)
   - Center-aligned text
   - Maximum readability

5. Audio Mix:
   - Voiceover: Clear, prominent in center channel
   - Music: Supporting role, -6dB to -10dB below VO
   - Clean transitions between segments
   - No abrupt cuts in music
