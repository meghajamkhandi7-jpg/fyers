
# 2026 Shipping Cost Analysis Summary

## Executive Summary
I conducted a comprehensive analysis of flat rate shipping costs across USPS, FedEx, and UPS to determine the most cost-effective carrier for a Nevada-based merchandising company shipping to U.S. consumers.

## Key Findings

### 1. Historical Rate Increases (2020-2025)
- **2020**: 4.9% (all carriers)
- **2021**: 4.9% (FedEx/UPS), USPS 2.5-4.2%
- **2022**: 6.3% (carriers average)
- **2023**: 6.9% (all carriers)
- **2024**: 5.9% (all carriers)
- **2025**: 5.9% (all carriers)

**Average Annual Increase**: 5.28%

### 2. 2025 Current Commercial Rates (per package)

**Pak/Envelope:**
- USPS: $8.40 (Priority Mail Flat Rate Envelope)
- FedEx: $15.25 (One Rate Pak)
- UPS: Does not offer pak format

**Small Box:**
- USPS: $11.70
- FedEx: $18.25
- UPS: $34.85

**Medium Box:**
- USPS: $21.05
- FedEx: $22.75
- UPS: $39.05

**Large Box:**
- USPS: $27.10
- FedEx: $29.95
- UPS: $58.60

**Extra Large Box:**
- USPS: Does not offer
- FedEx: $39.95
- UPS: $75.00

### 3. 2026 Total Projected Costs (6,060 shipments)

- **USPS Only** (limited services): $99,120.07
- **UPS Only** (all services): $213,512.05
- **FedEx Only** (all services): $132,618.06
- **Recommended Mix**: $104,167.19

## Recommendations

### Optimal Carrier by Package Size:
1. **Pak/Envelope**: USPS - $8.88 per unit → $8,865 total
2. **Small Box**: USPS - $12.32 per unit → $28,325 total
3. **Medium Box**: USPS - $22.16 per unit → $46,534 total
4. **Large Box**: USPS - $28.53 per unit → $15,406 total
5. **Extra Large Box**: FedEx - $42.06 per unit → $5,048 total

## Potential Savings
- Using recommended mix vs. UPS only: **$109,344.86 savings**
- Using recommended mix vs. FedEx only: **$28,450.87 savings**

## Implementation Note
The company should establish accounts with both USPS (for sizes Small through Large plus Pak) and FedEx (for Extra Large boxes) to maximize cost efficiency in 2026.
